● Proposed — Next.js

Proposed structure

Clean, extensionless, lowercase, non-www, ≤ 2 levels. Typed & siloed by axis; every leaf generated from in-repo data. English at root, /zh reserved.

Root & global

  • / Home
  • /about
  • /promotions
  • /contact
  • /privacy · /terms

Doctors NEW axis

  • /doctors E-E-A-T roster hub
  • /doctors/[doctor-slug] dr-jane-tan

Treatments

  • /treatments pillar hub
  • /treatments/[slug]
  • hifu · ultherapy · pico-laser · coolsculpting · fat-freezing · fotona-4d · onda · microneedling · radiofrequency · dermav · bio-stimulator · skin-booster · exosome-therapy · tattoo-removal · double-eyelid

Concerns NEW axis

  • /concerns pillar hub
  • /concerns/skin · /concerns/face categories
  • /concerns/[slug]
  • acne · aging · pigmentation · enlarged-pores · dark-eye-circles · face-contouring · face-lifting · fine-lines-wrinkles · body-slimming · hair-loss

Locations

  • /locations crawlable locator hub
  • /locations/[branch-slug]
  • mont-kiara · cheras · bukit-jalil · four-seasons-kl · petaling-jaya · kota-kemuning · southkey-johor-bahru · pelangi-johor-bahru · kota-kinabalu

Blog on-domain

  • /blog index / hub
  • /blog/[slug] 1:1 from blog.kaiteki.my
  • /blog/category/[c] optional · noindex,follow

System & i18n

  • /sitemap.xml index → per-type
  • /sitemaps/[type].xml · /robots.txt
  • 404 real status
  • /zh/** reserved mirror (EN unprefixed)

What changed docs/04 §1

Flat → typed & siloedOne route group per axis, each with its own data & sitemap segment. No more ~80 loose files.
Extensions removed.html/.php → clean, lowercase, hyphenated, extensionless paths.
Two new axesDedicated /concerns and /doctors — a competitor gap and the E-E-A-T moat.
Blog consolidatedblog.kaiteki.my subdomain → /blog subfolder to reclaim domain authority.
Branch slug fixedLegacy /kuala-lumpur.php actually served Bukit Jalil — corrected to /locations/bukit-jalil.
i18n-readyLocale-neutral slugs; EN at root, /zh (later /ms) purely additive — no geo baked into slugs.
